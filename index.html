Slaughterhouse

History

This is a story set in the fictional city of Sant’Angelo, an old, yet relatively stable settlement in the East Coast of the United States of America. You usually perform your duties alongside John, your mentor and leader of the agency, who hand-picked you out of several candidates given your potential.

Most days come and go with relatively few noteworthy occasions: an arrest here and there, some shocking discoveries and a few happy endings. While some at the agency would label these days “boring”, you find them at least interesting.

This uneventfulness continues until one fateful day, when you’re given a case that could very well change your life forever... or end it altogether.
